### The Siren

| Integrity | Hit Modifier | Defend Modifier | Weakness                                 | Resistance                                                                            |

#### Actions
* 6 rending as you swing claws
* 4 impact range Summon Undead as you throw a thrall spirit
* All in Earshot, Panic as you scream

#### Traits
* Drag to the depths - when The Siren goes to 0 hp its face opens showing a distended horrorful jaw. It can now bite for 8 damage. if this includes injury the person begins to be swallowed.
