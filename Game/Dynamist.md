# Dynamist

Character who specializes in manipulation of energy and the elements. Creating fire, storms, magical barriers, or disrupting the healing of a troll.

Elementalists are:

- Pyromancers
- Storm Callers
- Sorcerers
- Wizards
- Elementalist
