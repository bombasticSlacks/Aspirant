### Book Worm
*You always have your nose in a book.*
* You can read a book in an incredibly short amount of time.
* You memorize any words or images you see.
* If someone references a text once per [[Telling-The-Story#Downtime]] you may declare that you have already read it (and to function as a living book able to recite it).
