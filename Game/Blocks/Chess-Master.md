### Chess Master
*You are always attempting to stay one step ahead of enemies.*
* You choose [[Combat#Who Goes First]] in [[Combat]].
* You may perform a **command** without spending **authority** at the beginning of combat before the first [[Terminology#Character]].
* When another [[Terminology#Player Character]] has something happen to them your character is not aware of, you can spend an **authority** to intuit their current situation and have full knowledge of it.
