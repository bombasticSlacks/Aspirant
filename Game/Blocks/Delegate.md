### Delegate
*You can push your allies to act at a moments notice and rely on them to perform.*
* After you perform an [[Terminology#Action]] you may spend an **authority** to have a non-[[Terminology#Player Character]] [[Terminology#Ally]] perform an [[Terminology#Action]]. Gain [[Exerted]].
* You may spend **authority** when you remove [[Exerted]] to have all allies remove [[Exerted]].
