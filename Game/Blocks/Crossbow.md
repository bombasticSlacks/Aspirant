### Crossbow
*Your weapon fires bolts of metal.*
* Adds 3 times size to damage bonus instead of 2 times.
* Range of [[Movement#Short]].
* [[Capacity]] 1([[Munitions#Bolt]]).
* Gains [[Salvageable-Ammo]].
* Loses [[Striking]] and gains [[Targeted]].
