### Reaction
*You can get ahead of things.*
You may spend **influence** to interrupt or step back an [[Terminology#Action]] that has already occurred, or is in the middle of occurring.

> If someone had just pulled a lever to lower a drawbridge, you could spend an **influence** to attack them before they can do it, hopefully, leaving the bridge up.
