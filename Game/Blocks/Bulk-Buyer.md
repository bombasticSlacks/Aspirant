### Bulk Buyer
*You can take advantage of fluctuations in local markets.*
* Whenever you enter a new place the [[Terminology#Game Master]] will decide a [[Materials]] that the settlement has an abundance of, and one it is lacking. Unless something drastic changes about the world these will be consistent every time you visit this place.
* Once per visit in a place you may:
	* Purchase the abundant material at a 10% discount (rounding total cost up).
	* Sell the lacking material at a 10% markup (rounding total cost down).
