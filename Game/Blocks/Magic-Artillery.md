### Magical Artillery

Anyone targeted by your attacks with the [Magical-Attacks](Game/Core/Magical-Attacks) trait suffers a (-1) to all [Actions](Core/Terminology#Action) until your next [Combat Turn](Core/Terminology#Combat%20Turn).
