#### Exotic Food
*Food made with intentional specialized processes to make it tasty and last.*

* Cost 2 for 1.
* 1 unit provides food for a day. 
* If you eat only Exotic Food for an entire [[Telling-The-Story#Travel Segment]] you reduce 1 [[Stress]].
