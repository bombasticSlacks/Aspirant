#### Basic Food
*Dry prepared food, unappealing and filling.*

* Cost 3 for 5.
* 1 unit provides food for a day but if you eat it during a [Travel Segment](Game/Telling-The-Story#Travel%20Segment) you gain 1 [Stress](Game/Core/Blocks/Stress).
